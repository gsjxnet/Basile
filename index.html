WELCOME TO THE ZAMASA FOUNDATION

The Zamasa Foundation has been established as a dedicated medical research foundation focused specifically on leading edge research into multiple myeloma, with an emphasis on recent genetically engineered advances in immunotherapy. The Foundation will also focus on the advancement of genetic approaches to treatments that are focused on some of the more debilitating aspects associated with disease progression (such as bone and kidney damage). Initiatives targeting more effective early screening of MM may also be considered.
