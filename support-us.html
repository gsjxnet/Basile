Fundraising

The ZAMASA Foundation Committee is responsible for raising the requisite funding to support immunotherapy advances as they apply to multiple myeloma. A priority of the Committee is to ensure sustainable funding and to this end fundraising will target individual (including bequests) and corporate donors, event sponsorship and community fundraising.

From time to time the ZAMASA Foundation may undertake to support in full or part a Major Project. A Major Project is defined as a specific fund raising initiative dedicated to achieving a specific purpose. This will be outside the annual funding activities that support the general Objects of the Foundation and the Committee will establish a robust funding plan to support the specific project.
