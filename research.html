Applying for Funding

The ZAMASA Foundation directs its financial support to targeted immunology research aimed at improving outcomes and quality of life for patients diagnosed with multiple myeloma. While not exhaustive, research areas of current interest include:

  • Monoclonal antibodies, including bi-specific antibodies and antibody conjugates;
  • Chimeric antigen receptors (CAR) T cell and other types of engineered T cells;
  • Allogenic CAR T transplants;
  • Allogenic bone marrow transplants; and,
  • Myeloma vaccines.

Initial interest in possible funding support from the ZAMASA Foundation should be addressed to Mr. Greg Arandt and Associate Professor John Moore
